# Dialysis Care Plan Taskboard - User Guide

## 🩺 What is this app?
The **Dialysis Care Plan Taskboard** is a comprehensive management dashboard designed for medical staff (nurses, dieticians, doctors) treating patients receiving dialysis. It helps organize, prioritize, and track medical care tasks so no treatment steps are missed.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Patient Cards
Lists patients undergoing care with their details (Name, Ward, Age). Each patient header includes a button to add tasks immediately.

### 2. Dynamic Taskboard columns
Tasks are structured into **three live pipelines** according to status:
*   🔴 **Overdue**: Critical tasks that missed their due date.
*   🟡 **In Progress**: Active tasks currently ongoing.
*   🟢 **Completed**: Solved care items.

### 3. Powerful Filtering
The navigation bar at the top lets you filter the board view:
*   **By Role**: See only your discipline's tasks (e.g., `nurse`, `dietician`).
*   **By Time**: Scope the view to `Today` or `This Week` to plan your shift quickly.

---

## 📖 How it Works (User Instructions)

### 👤 1. Browsing the Board
When you load the application:
1.  All patients are loaded in separate sections.
2.  You can easily scan task cards to see:
    *   **Task Title**: e.g. *"Check Blood Pressure"*
    *   **Assignee**: Staff member dealing with it.
    *   **Role Tag**: Which team it belongs to.
    *   **Due Date**: Time sensitivity.

### ➕ 2. Adding a Task
1.  Navigate to the patient card you are dealing with.
2.  Click the blue **+ Add Task** button in their header.
3.  Fill out the Modal Form with:
    *   **Title**: Clear description (e.g., *"Assess weight fluid gain"*)
    *   **Role**: Responsible team (nurse, doctor, etc.)
    *   **Assignee**: Name of the staff member.
    *   **Due Date**: Specify when it's due.
4.  Click **Create Task**. It will auto-populate into your list.
